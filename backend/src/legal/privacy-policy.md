## 1. Information we collect

**a) Information you give us**
- **Account:** phone number and/or email, password (stored hashed).
- **Profile:** name, date of birth (to confirm 18+ and show age), gender and who you'd like to meet, profession, photos, bio/prompts, height, education, and similar profile details you choose to add.
- **Location:** your city and, if you grant permission, precise GPS coordinates (used to show nearby people and distance — see §4).
- **Verification:** if you request profession verification, documents/links you submit.
- **Content:** messages you send to matches, and reports you file about other users.
- **Support:** anything you send us by email or in support requests.

**b) Information collected automatically**
- **Usage & device data:** app interactions, device model, OS, app version, IP address, and diagnostic/log data.
- **Approximate location** may be inferred from IP even without GPS permission.

**c) From third parties**
- **Payments:** when you buy Pro or credits, Apple's App Store or Google Play processes the payment and confirms it to us. **We never receive your full card details.**

We do **not** buy your personal data from data brokers.

## 2. How we use your information
- Operate the core service: build your profile, run same-/cross-profession matching, and show distance.
- Safety, moderation, fraud prevention, and enforcing our Terms.
- Profession verification (best-effort; see the Terms).
- Process purchases and manage your Pro/credits entitlements.
- Send you match, message, and account notifications (you can control these).
- Provide support and respond to you.
- Comply with law and protect our rights and users.

## 3. How we share your information
- **With other users:** your profile (photos, name, profession, city, and — for people nearby — approximate distance) is shown to other members as part of the service. **We do not share your exact GPS coordinates or contact details with other users.**
- **Service providers** who run the App on our behalf: cloud hosting, database, push notifications, and the app-store payment processors (Apple, Google). They may only use data to provide their service to us.
- **Legal & safety:** to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the safety, rights, or property of users, the public, or Wovnn.
- **Business transfers:** if Wovnn is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

**We do not sell your personal information.**

## 4. Location
Precise location is optional and used to show people near you and how far away matches are. We show others only a coarse distance/area, never your exact coordinates. You can turn off location any time in your device settings; some nearby features won't work without it, but the App still functions.

## 5. Your choices and rights
- **Access / correct:** view and edit your profile in the App.
- **Delete:** you can permanently delete your account in the App (this erases your profile, matches, and messages); or **deactivate** to hide temporarily.
- **Location & notifications:** control via your device settings.
- Depending on where you live (e.g., **India's DPDP Act**, **EU/UK GDPR**, **California CCPA**), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, port, or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Contact wovnnsupport@gmail.com to exercise these. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority.

## 6. Data retention
We keep your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal data, except where we must retain limited records for legal, safety, fraud-prevention, or accounting reasons.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organisational measures (e.g., hashed passwords, encrypted transport) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children
Wovnn is strictly for adults **18 and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18; if we learn a user is under 18, we will remove the account.

## 9. International transfers
Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country (e.g., our cloud provider's regions). Where required, we take steps to protect it in accordance with applicable law.
